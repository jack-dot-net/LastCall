Liar's Bar

Bluff. Call. Spin the chamber.

or
How to play

Deck: 6 Kings, 6 Queens, 6 Aces, 2 Jokers (Jokers are wild).

Each round a "table card" is announced (e.g. King). Everyone is dealt 5 cards.

On your turn, either:

  • Play 1–3 cards face down, claiming they are all the table card (or Jokers).
  • Call LIAR! on the previous player.

If you call LIAR, the last cards played are revealed.

  • If they were lying, they pull the trigger.
  • If they were truthful, you pull the trigger.

Each gun has 6 chambers and 1 bullet at a random position. The chamber advances with every shot — it gets more dangerous the longer you live.

Last one breathing wins the bar.
