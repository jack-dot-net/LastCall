How to play

Three modes, one revolver. Whichever you pick, the loser of a LIAR call pulls the trigger on their own gun (6 chambers, 1 bullet, chamber advances every shot). Last one breathing wins.

Liar's Cards. Deck of Kings, Queens, Aces, and 2 wild Jokers. Each round picks a table card. On your turn, play 1–3 cards face down claiming they match — or call LIAR.

Liar's Dice. Everyone rolls 5 hidden dice. Bid total quantity × face across all dice. Each next bid must increase (higher qty, or same qty higher face). 1s are wild (count as any face). Call LIAR to reveal.

Bluff Poker. Each player is dealt 5 cards. Declare a poker hand (pair, two pair, three, straight, flush, full house, quads, straight flush) of strictly increasing rank — claiming it exists across everyone's combined cards. Call LIAR and reveal all.
