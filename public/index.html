How to play

Rules

Each round, everyone receives five hidden cards. The table names a claim rank: Crows, Moons, or Keys. On your turn, play one to three cards face down and claim they match the rank. Ember cards are wild.

The next player may play their own claim or call bluff. If the revealed cards match the claim, the caller loses a life. If any card fails, the bluffer loses a life.

Players start with three lives. Lose them all and you become a spectator. The last surviving player wins Last Call.
